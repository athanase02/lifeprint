Rwanda Leads Africa in Digital Health Innovation with LifePrint Platform

The Rwanda Ministry of Health has officially recognized LifePrint as a key digital health partner in the national wellness strategy. With over 15,000 active users across all 30 districts, LifePrint is transforming how Rwandans approach preventive health care. The platform's PersonaPrint assessment has helped identify early warning signs in 3,200 users, leading to timely medical interventions. Health Minister Dr. Sabin Nsanzimana praised the platform's integration of traditional Rwandan wellness practices with modern AI technology.
