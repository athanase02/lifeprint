Common Questions

Everything you need to know about LifePrint

What makes LifePrint different from other health apps?

LifePrint stands out by combining personalized health tracking with AI-driven insights and community support. Unlike generic health apps, we use the PersonaPrint assessment to create a unique health profile for each user, ensuring recommendations are tailored to your specific needs, lifestyle, and goals. Our platform integrates physical health, mental wellness, and social connection in one comprehensive ecosystem.

How secure is my health data?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. All health information is stored in HIPAA-compliant servers, and we never sell or share your personal data with third parties. You maintain complete control over what information you share and with whom.

What is the PersonaPrint assessment?

PersonaPrint is our comprehensive health assessment tool that evaluates multiple dimensions of your wellbeing including physical health, mental state, lifestyle habits, and personal goals. It takes about 10-15 minutes to complete and generates a detailed profile that helps our AI provide personalized recommendations. The assessment adapts over time as you progress and your needs evolve.

Can I control who sees my health information?

Absolutely! You have granular control over your privacy settings. You can choose to keep your profile completely private, share specific metrics with friends or family, or join community challenges while keeping personal details hidden. Your health stories and achievements can be shared selectively, and you can revoke access at any time.

Is LifePrint free to use?

LifePrint offers a generous free tier that includes basic health tracking, the PersonaPrint assessment, and community features. Our Premium plan ($9.99/month or $89.99/year) unlocks advanced AI insights, detailed analytics, unlimited time capsule entries, priority support, and exclusive wellness content. We also offer a free 30-day trial of Premium for new users.

What devices and platforms does LifePrint support?

LifePrint is available as a web application accessible from any modern browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices. We also offer native iOS and Android apps for the best mobile experience. The platform syncs seamlessly across all your devices, so you can track your progress anywhere, anytime.

How does the AI-powered health tracking work?

Our AI analyzes your health data, activity patterns, and PersonaPrint profile to provide intelligent insights and recommendations. It learns from your behaviors and progress over time, identifying trends, predicting potential health risks, and suggesting personalized actions. The AI also helps set realistic goals and adjusts recommendations based on your achievements and challenges.

What are Time Capsule Goals?

Time Capsule Goals are a unique feature that lets you set long-term health intentions and "seal" them for a future date. Write a letter to your future self, set health milestones, and document your journey. When the time capsule "opens" at your chosen date, you'll receive your message along with progress analytics, creating a powerful reflection moment on your health transformation.

Can I integrate LifePrint with other health apps and devices?

Yes! LifePrint integrates with popular fitness trackers and health apps including Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and more. This allows automatic syncing of your activity data, sleep patterns, and other health metrics. You can also manually log data or import health records from other sources.

How can I get support if I have issues?

We offer multiple support channels: in-app chat support (available 24/7), email support at support@lifeprint.com, comprehensive help center with tutorials and guides, and an active community forum where users and experts share advice. Premium members also get priority support with faster response times.
